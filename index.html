About

I am an assistant professor at the Federal Institute of São Paulo (Brazil) and a Ph.D. student in Medical Image Analysis at the University of Campinas (Brazil) and University of Groninger (the Netherlands). A very passionate professor, researcher, boardgamer, and broadcaster.
